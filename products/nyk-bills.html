Offline Billing & Accounting Software

Nyk Bills is a Windows‑only offline billing solution built with React and Vite – ideal for small and medium businesses that need fast GST / Non‑GST invoicing without a cloud database or complex setup.

System Requirements

  • • Windows 10 or Windows 11 (64‑bit only)
  • • Minimum 4 GB RAM (8 GB recommended)
  • • 500 MB free disk space for app & data
  • • .NET / runtime dependencies bundled with installer
  • • No internet connection required after activation

Key Advantages

  • Offline billing – no online database or server required.
  • Create both GST and Non‑GST invoices with flexible tax settings.
  • Fast invoice printing with clean, professional layouts.
  • Inventory management: track products, quantities, and rates.
  • Client management: maintain customer master with contact details.
  • Purchase entry tracking for basic in/out stock control.

Designed For

  • Small and medium businesses needing quick desktop billing.
  • Shops that want offline safety (no cloud dependency).
  • Users who prefer simple accounting workflows over complex ERPs.
  • Institutes or local vendors with basic GST / Non‑GST needs.
